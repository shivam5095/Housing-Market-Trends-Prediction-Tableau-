About This Project

Understanding the Housing Market Trends Analysis

Project Overview (ABC Company)

In this project for ABC Company, the goal is to address challenges in understanding the factors that influence house prices and sales trends. By analyzing comprehensive housing data—including the total sales by years since renovation, house age distribution by structural features, and the impact of renovations—the company aims to uncover key insights.

Target Audience

Key stakeholders include real estate analysts, marketing teams, and company executives, all of whom will benefit from a deeper understanding of housing market dynamics to inform strategic decisions, optimize pricing strategies, and enhance overall market competitiveness.

Analysis Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: Overall Data Overview – A foundational understanding of the dataset's scale and key metrics.
  • Scenario 2: Sales by Years Since Renovation – Understanding the impact of renovations on house prices.
  • Scenario 3: Distribution of House Age – Assessing age characteristics and renovation prevalence.
  • Scenario 4: Structural Features – Identifying patterns related to bathrooms, bedrooms, and floors over time.
